🔥 23 Years. 7 Regions. 1 Climate Warning.

I decoded NASA POWER climate data (2001–2024) across 7 climate-vulnerable regions — and the results are alarming. Here’s what the Earth is telling us:

🌍 Regions Analyzed

  • Heatburg – Similar to Arizona desert (extreme heat)
  • Sunblaze Valley – Similar to California's Coachella Valley (heat waves)
  • Mirage Plains – Similar to Rajasthan, India (extreme temperatures)
  • Ember Delta – Similar to Bolivia Altiplano (high solar radiation)
  • Droughtford – Similar to South Australia (arid climate)
  • Flame Oasis – Similar to Egypt's Eastern Desert (high temperatures)
  • Scorch Basin – Similar to Shandong, China (heat waves)

🌡️ Heat is Surging

  • Heatburg hit 49.5°C (2023)
  • Sunblaze Valley lost its cold buffer — warmer winters
  • Scorch Basin swings from -18.0°C to over 40°C

☀️ UV is Off the Charts

  • Heatburg: 1000+ days UV Index >6
  • Droughtford & Ember Delta face year-round UV stress

💧 Rain is Vanishing

  • Flame Oasis: 0.1% rain chance
  • Scorch Basin: sharp drop from 8.7% → 3.5%
  • Droughtford: lost half its rain probability since 2001

💨 Wind vs. Heat

  • Wind = relief in Ember Delta, but desiccates Scorch Basin
  • Mirage Plains needs low wind + high humidity for any relief

🚨 Climate Risk Zones Identified

  • 🔥 Extreme Heat: Heatburg, Sunblaze Valley
  • 🆘 Aid Priority: Ember Delta, Flame Oasis
  • ☢️ Radiation Exposure: Droughtford, Scorch Basin

📊 How I Did It

  • Tools: Python + NASA POWER API
  • Analysis: Jupyter Notebook
  • Methods: Theil-Sen trend estimation, 5-year moving averages
  • Visuals: Temperature timelines, UV heatmaps, Rainfall trends, Wind-heat scatterplots

⚡ Why It Matters

  • 🌡️ Human health risks are real
  • ☀️ Solar potential is huge
  • 🌾 Agriculture must adapt — or fail
